Burritos

Description

I love my carbs, and one of the best dishes to get them are burritos! They are easy to make, tasty, and portable, it would be difficult to find a better alternative

I hate when I'm reading a recipe and it asks for some obscure ingredient like "a petal from a flower that was planted in June of 2012". So we're going to be using simple ingredients that you can find at your local grocery store.

Ingredients

For this dish, you will need:

  • flour tortillas
  • canned black beans
  • spices
  • avocados
  • tomatoes
  • cooked rice
  • corn

Steps

  1. Drain the corn and the black beans
  2. Dice the tomatoes
  3. Mash the avocados (yes, I said mash them. Thank me later.)
  4. Heat a pan with some olive oil, and fry the black beans until hot. Season them with whatever spices you want and have at hand. Have some fun!
  5. Finally, heat the tortillas in the microwave or a pan (optional), put the rice, beans, avocado, tomatoes and corn in it, try to fold it (I'm no expert here, so I won't judge your form), and enjoy!
