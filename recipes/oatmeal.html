Oatmeal

Description

The humble oatmeal is a versatile meal that can be done quickly and eaten at any time. Breakfast? No problem. Lunch? It might be weird, but go for it! Dinner? You can totally do it.

In this version, fruits and some seeds will be used, making it a nutritional bomb.

Ingredients

You can get really creative, but this is what I usually use for mine:

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 spoon chia seeds
  • 1 spoon flax seeds
  • 1/2 spoon pumpkin seeds
  • raisins
  • 1 banana
  • Any other fruits you want to add
  • 1 cup of water or plant milk
  • cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add the oats, seeds, raisins, and cinnamon, to a small pot.
  2. Add the water/plan milk to the pot. Stir and bring to a boil.
  3. Once it starts boiling, set the heat to low and continue cooking for 3-5 minutes.
  4. In the meantime, cut the banana in half. Slice one half and mash the other.
  5. Chop any other fruit that needs it (e.g. strawberries)
  6. When the oats are done, serve them in a bowl and mix with the mashed banana. You can add the rest of the fruits on top or mix them if you prefer (I certainly do it!).
