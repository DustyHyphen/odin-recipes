Refried Beans

Description

Refried beans are amazing as a dip, or to add to your homemade burritos.

As usual, we will be using common ingredients you are likely to already have at home.

Ingredients

For this easy recipe, all you will need is:

  • canned beans (I usually use black ones)
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 1/4 pepper (any kind)
  • garlic (only you know how much is too much)
  • spices

Steps

  1. Pre-heat a pan with some olive oil.
  2. Chop the onion, pepper, garlic, and carrot.
  3. Add the chopped veggies to the pan, and fry until they are tender.
  4. Add the full can of beans to the mix.
  5. add your spices and stir for a couple of minutes.
  6. When the beans are hot, mashed them until the desired consistency is reached.
  7. That's it! Serve hot and enjoy.
